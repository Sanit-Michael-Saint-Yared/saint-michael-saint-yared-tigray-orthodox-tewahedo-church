Welcome

Welcome to the official website of the Saint Michael and Saint Yared Tigray Orthodox Tewahedo Church in Baltimore. Our mission is to teach and preach the Christian faith grounded in the Tewahedo tradition. Join us every Sunday and on all Holy Days to celebrate worship, community, and culture.
